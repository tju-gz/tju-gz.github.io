# 🙋‍♂️ About Me 关于我
Hi! I am Zheng Gong, an Associate Researcher at the [College of Intelligence and Computing](https://cic.tju.edu.cn/) at [Tianjin University](https://www.tju.edu.cn/)(天津大学智能与计算学部/网络安全学院). I am also a researcher at [TANKLab](https://tj.teacher.360eol.com/teacherBasic/preview?teacherId=12111), led by [Prof. Keqiu Li](https://cic.tju.edu.cn/faculty/likeqiu/), IEEE Fellow. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/)🇭🇰 in February 2025, supervised by [Prof. Lei Yang](https://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~csyanglei/#/pages/profile/about). Before that, I received my Master's Degree at Xidian University, supervised by [Prof. Hui Li](https://lihuidb.com/). My research spans the fields of low-power communication and sensing systems. 

I have published full research papers on <span style="display: inline-block; background-color: 	#208817; color: white; padding: 1.5px 6px; border-radius: 3px; font-weight: bold; line-height: 100%;">ALL CCF A conferences</span> in computer networks, including <span style="display: inline-block; background-color: #eb2513; color: white; padding: 1.5px 6px; border-radius: 3px; font-weight: bold; line-height: 100%;">ACM SIGCOMM</span>, <span style="display: inline-block; background-color: #eb2513; color: white; padding: 1.5px 6px; border-radius: 3px; font-weight: bold; line-height: 100%;">ACM MobiCom</span>, <span style="display: inline-block; background-color: #eb2513; color: white; padding: 1.5px 6px; border-radius: 3px; font-weight: bold; line-height: 100%;">USENIX NSDI</span>, and <span style="display: inline-block; background-color: #208817; color: white; padding: 1.5px 6px; border-radius: 3px; font-weight: bold; line-height: 100%;">IEEE INFOCOM</span>. My work has been awarded the <span style="display: inline-block; background-color: #ce1e27; color: white; padding: 1.5px 6px; border-radius: 3px; font-weight: bold; line-height: 100%;">Best Demo Award Runner-Up</span> at ACM MobiCom 2022.
